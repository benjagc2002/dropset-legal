Privacy Policy — DropSet

Last updated: June 12, 2026

DropSet is a workout randomizer with a collection mechanic, built by an

independent developer based in Chile. This policy explains, in plain

language and with no fine print, what data the app handles, what for,

and how you can delete it.

Contact for any privacy matter: gonzaliflags@gmail.com.

The essentials, in one line

No ads, no data selling, no third-party analytics. We store the minimum

needed so your collection and progress follow you across devices, and

you can delete it all from inside the app at any time.

What data we collect

By default you use DropSet with an anonymous session: a random user

identifier is created that does not reveal who you are. If you decide

to sign up (it is optional), we add your email so you can recover

your account.

Specifically, we store:

  • User identifier: a random id that links your data together.
  • Email: only if you sign up voluntarily.
  • Completed-workout records: date and session performed.
  • Coins and collectibles: the ledger of what you have earned by

training.

  • Training preferences: available equipment, duration, level.

That is all. We do not access your contacts, location, photos,

microphone, or any other data on your device.

What we do NOT do with your data

  • We show no advertising of any kind.
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We use no third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Facebook

SDK, no trackers).

  • We do not profile your behavior for commercial purposes.

Who processes your data

We use Supabase Inc. as a data processor: it hosts the database

where the data described above lives, following our instructions and

without using it for its own purposes.

Payments

The premium subscription is purchased and processed through the

App Store (Apple) or Google Play (Google), with subscription

management by RevenueCat. Payment is processed directly by Apple or

Google: we never see or store your card details. From RevenueCat we

only receive your subscription status (active or not) to unlock the

premium features.

How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you

delete your account, everything is erased immediately and irreversibly.

We do not keep "just in case" copies.

How to delete your data

No emails, no forms, no waiting:

1. Open DropSet.

2. Go to Profile → Delete account.

3. Confirm. Deletion is immediate and irreversible: user id, email,

workouts, coins, collectibles, and preferences are gone.

If you prefer, you can also write to gonzaliflags@gmail.com and we

will handle it for you.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: know what data of yours we hold.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: erase all your data (in-app or by email).

To exercise them, use the in-app deletion or write to

gonzaliflags@gmail.com. We respond at no cost and with no hurdles.

Legal framework

We process your data in accordance with **Chile's Law 19.628 on the

Protection of Private Life**. If you are a European Union user, we

additionally apply the principles of the GDPR: lawful basis, data

minimization, right to be forgotten, and portability. The basis that

legitimizes the processing is the performance of the service you

request by using the app (and your consent, in the case of the optional

email).

Children

DropSet is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly

collect data from children under that age; if you believe it has

happened, write to us and we will delete it immediately.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you inside the app before

the change takes effect, with a clear summary of what changes. We will

never lower your guarantees without telling you.
